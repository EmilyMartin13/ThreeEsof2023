I got accepted into the LVSA (Loire Valley Study Aboard) program in October of 2023. I was so incredibly excited to get the opportunity to travel to Europe and learn more about the bible with other young adult Christians.

My flight to Paris had a layover in Toronto. Here, I met 5 other Canadian girls who were also in the program.

France immediately had my heart. Gorgeous, incredibly detailed buildings and amazing baked goods. What more could you want?
